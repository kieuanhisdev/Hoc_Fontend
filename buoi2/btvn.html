Cau 1: Bai tap ve the video

Yeu cau: tao thu muc dat ten la video, sau do lay video bat ky vao thu muc videos do. Tiep theo, chen video do vao trang web bang cach su dung the video va the source. Duong dan la video la dang relative (Duong dan tuong doi). Dat kich thuoc video co width bang 720 va height bang 405 (tuong duong ty le 16/9)
